We produce forgings according to customer's requirements. The production process is open-die forging. Weight of forgings up to 12 tons for carbon steel forgings and up to 7 tons for stainless steel forgings. We also offer titanium forgings. Any cross-sections of forgings: forgings of rods, forgings of rings, disks, plates, etc.

We offer high quality forging material through the use of high quality vacuum steel ingots. We also offer heat-treated forgings with the required mechanical properties. Machining of forgings is carried out on lathes and boring machines. In our testing laboratory we provide macro and micro tests, determination of mechanical properties at high temperatures and low temperatures. You can calculate the weight and cost, as well as order the required forging. This form determines configures the forging automatically (disk, ring or coupling for round forgings, and cube or bar for square forgings).
